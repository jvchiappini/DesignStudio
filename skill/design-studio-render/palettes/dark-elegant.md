## Gradient Formulas for Dark Palettes

For `bgStyle` attribute:

```jsx
bgStyle="linear-gradient(135deg, #0a0a1a, #1a1a3e)"
bgStyle="radial-gradient(circle at 50% 30%, #1a1a3e 0%, #0a0a1a 100%)"
bgStyle="linear-gradient(180deg, #0a1628 0%, #0a0a1a 100%)"
bgStyle="linear-gradient(135deg, #1a0a0f 0%, #0a0a1a 100%)"
```
